Electrical Basics

  • Know your panel: label breakers; test outlets with GFCI button.
  • Replace outlets/switches: power off, verify no voltage, match wire colors, secure box.
  • Lighting: support fixtures properly; cap unused wires with wire nuts.
  • Use proper gauge and connectors; never overload circuits.

When unsure, hire a licensed electrician.
