FAQ

When should I call a pro?

Gas lines, main electrical, structural work, and complex roofing — always hire licensed professionals.

How often to repaint?

Interior walls: 3–5 years; trim/doors: 5–7 years depending on wear.

What’s the best all-purpose sealant?

Paintable acrylic-latex for indoor gaps; silicone for wet areas.
