Plumbing Basics

  • Stop a Leak

    Shut water, dry area, use PTFE tape on threads, re-tighten gently.

  • Clogged Drain

    Plunger first, then drain snake. Avoid harsh chemicals.

  • Replace P-Trap

    Bucket under trap, loosen slip nuts, swap gasket, hand-tighten then 1/4 turn.

  • Seal with Caulk

    Cut at 45°, steady pressure, smooth with wet finger/tool.
