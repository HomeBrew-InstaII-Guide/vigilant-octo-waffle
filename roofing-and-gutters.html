Roof & Gutters

  • Clean gutters spring & autumn; ensure downspouts discharge away from foundation.
  • Inspect shingles for curling, missing tabs, or exposed nails.
  • Seal small flashing gaps with roof sealant; call pros for structural damage.
  • Use fall protection and never work on wet/icy roofs.
